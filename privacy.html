01 Overview

Shared Experiences ("the App") is an iOS application developed and operated by RSDH ("we", "us", or "our"). The App is designed to help you store, organise, and view travel documents such as reservations and itineraries.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how it is stored, and what rights you have in relation to your information.

In plain terms: The App does not collect or transmit personal data to our servers. Your travel documents remain on your device and, if you choose, within your personal iCloud account.

02 Information We Collect

Information you provide

The App allows you to store travel documents (such as booking confirmations, flight itineraries, and hotel reservations) that you choose to add. This content is stored locally on your device or synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit framework. We do not have access to this content.

Account & sign-in information

If you choose to sign in using Sign in with Apple, Apple may share a name and email address with the App solely for the purpose of creating and identifying your account. This information is used only to authenticate you within the App and is not stored on our servers.

We do not collect analytics data, advertising identifiers, location data, browsing history, or any other behavioural information.

Automatically collected information

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies within the App. Apple may collect certain technical data as part of standard iOS operation and App Store distribution; this is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

03 How We Use Your Information

The limited information described above is used solely to:

  • Authenticate and identify your account within the App (sign-in data only);
  • Enable iCloud sync of your travel documents across your personal Apple devices;
  • Respond to support enquiries you initiate with us.

We do not use your information for marketing, profiling, advertising, or any automated decision-making.

Where applicable under the UK GDPR or EU GDPR, our lawful basis for processing sign-in data is performance of a contract (Article 6(1)(b)) — specifically, to provide you access to the App's functionality.

04 Data Storage & Security

Your travel documents are stored:

  • On-device: locally within the App's sandboxed storage on your iPhone or iPad; or
  • iCloud: within your personal Apple iCloud account via CloudKit, if you have iCloud enabled for the App.

In both cases, your data is held within Apple's infrastructure and governed by Apple's security and privacy practices. We do not operate our own servers to store your travel documents.

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any information we do handle (e.g. support correspondence) against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

05 Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Apple Inc. — as your device manufacturer and iCloud provider, Apple processes data in accordance with their own privacy policy.
  • Legal obligations — if required to do so by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Protection of rights — where necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

06 Data Retention

Your travel documents are retained on your device or in iCloud for as long as you choose to keep the App installed or maintain iCloud sync. You may delete individual documents or all data at any time from within the App, or by deleting the App from your device.

Any support correspondence (e.g. emails sent to [email protected]) is retained for as long as necessary to resolve your enquiry and meet any legal obligations, after which it is securely deleted.
